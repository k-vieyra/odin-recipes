Chicken Pot Pie

Description

This recipe has quickly became one of my family's favorite dinners. The ingredients are kitchen staples, the directions are simple to follow, and the end result is divine.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 1 cups sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced celery
  • 1/2 cup yellow onion
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp dried flat leaf parsley
  • 2 pie crusts
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F and make sure there is a rack on the bottom rack.
  2. Shred 2 cups of chicken (I like to use the rotisserie chicken from Costco.
  3. Prepare the chopped vegetables.
  4. Melt 3 tbsp unsalted butter on medium heat and add the vegetables once the butter has melted. Add all of the seasonings. Cook for 8 minutes or until the onions are tender and translucent. Once cooked add your shredded chicken.
  5. Add the flour and mix until no dry flour is left. Mix in the chicken broth and heavy cream until it is bubbly and thick.
  6. Remove the pan from the heat. Stir in the peas and let sit for 15-30 minutes.
  7. Put one of the pie crusts into a 9 inch pie tin. Add the filling and cover the top with the remaining pie crust.
  8. Cut 3-4 slits in the top of the crust for ventilation.
  9. Put the pie on a baking sheet and transfer to the bottom rack of the oven. Bake for 30 minutes.
  10. Cool for 15-30 minutes before serving for best results.
