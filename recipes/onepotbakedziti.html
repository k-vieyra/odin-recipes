One Pot Baked Ziti

Description

This is quick and easy meal for any night of the week. It is cheesy and creamy...what more could you need?!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb hot Italian ground sausage
  • 14.5 oz can petite diced tomatoes
  • 8 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 6 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 3 cups water
  • 12 oz rigatoni, penne or ziti pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F.
  2. In a 6 quart cast iron, add olive oil over medium heat and cook the sausage until browned.
  3. Add in the minced garlic and red pepper flakes and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and the dried basil and oregano. Reduce heat to a simmer and let simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the water and dried pasta. Bring to a boil, cover and reduce the heat to a low boil. Cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the noodles are tender, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream, parmesan cheese and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Transfer the cast iron to the oven and bake for 5 minutes or until the cheese and melted and browned a tad.
  9. Enjoy!
